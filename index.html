Finding the park

The park sits along the SW Waterfront on M Place SW (also known as Eleanor Holmes Norton Park). Google Maps is a little confused about what it's called—please use the pin below rather than searching "Southwest Waterfront Park," which lands you closer to the Titanic Memorial (which is just a short walk away if you end up there!). The park is a five-minute walk from Waterfront Metro on the green line and there are two Capital Bikeshare stations nearby.
